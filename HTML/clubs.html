Student Clubs

Join Our Vibrant Club Community

At SkillsPark, we offer a diverse range of student-run clubs that cater to various interests and passions. Whether you're interested in technology, arts, sports, or academics, there's a club for you. Joining a club is a great way to meet like-minded peers, develop new skills, and enhance your college experience.

Academic Clubs

Computer Science Club

Weekly coding workshops, hackathons, and guest speakers from the tech industry.

Meets: Tuesdays, 4-6pm

Debate Society

Develop public speaking and critical thinking skills through competitive debate.

Meets: Wednesdays, 5-7pm

Science & Research Club

Hands-on experiments, research projects, and science fair preparation.

Meets: Thursdays, 3-5pm

Cultural & Arts Clubs

Art Club

Workshops, exhibitions, and creative projects for artists of all skill levels. Explore painting, sculpture, digital art, and collaborative installations.

Meets: Mondays, 6-8pm

Photography Society

Photo walks, technique workshops, and exhibition opportunities.

Meets: Saturdays, 10am-12pm

Special Interest Clubs

Robotics Club

Design, build, and program robots for competitions and research projects.

Meets: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 5-7pm

Entrepreneurship Society

Startup workshops, pitch competitions, and networking with entrepreneurs.

Meets: Mondays, 5-7pm

Start Your Own Club

Don't see a club that matches your interests? You can start your own! We provide resources, funding, and guidance to help you establish and grow your club. Contact the Student Activities Office to learn more about the process.
